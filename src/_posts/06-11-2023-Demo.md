## Demo of the Static Site Generator

## Text Formatting

You can format text in various ways:

- *Italic Text*
- **Bold Text**
- ***Bold and Italic Text***
- `Inline Code`

---

## Lists

Unordered Lists:

- Item 1
- Item 2
- Item 3

Ordered Lists:

1. First item
2. Second item
3. Third item

---

## Headings

You can create headings of different levels:
# Subheading = h1
## Subheading = h2
### Subheading = h3
#### Subheading = h4
##### Subheading = h5
